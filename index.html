The Spins

On Radio 1190

Listen up!

The Spins is an all vinyl show that features hand picked records from our combined collection. Each week we put together playlists relevant to what’s going on, what’s new, and our mood. We often have guests on our show who also love music! Radio 1190 is part of the University of Colorado and supports local and independent music. We are lucky to have an hour each week to share what we’ve been listening to, so tune in! For details about past shows or future announcements check below.

How can you listen?

We spin vinyl live every Friday from 8pm - 9pm MT. There are multiple ways to listen to the show:

  • Boulder residents:

    Tune in to 98.9FM

  • Boulder/Denver residents:

    Tune in to 1190AM

  • The World:

    Stream live at radio1190.org

Love vinyl too?

We would love to have you on our show. We also take requests! Please email brandon.miller@colorado.edu or tweet us @thespins1190.

December 9th - 2016

Winter is coming. Actually, it’s been here for quite some time now and Boulder is just starting to feel its wrath. . . This week we played, “Songs that remind us of winter and the cold”. We started out with the self titled album by the Fleet Foxes and made it all the way to bands like Minor Victories and Lo-Fang. We like to create playlists based off of our current moods or what’s relevant in our vinyl collection that week, so it’s safe to say these change often. Check out the entire playlist below and keep it around for days you feel like studying or cozying up with some warm beverages-it’s a good one!
